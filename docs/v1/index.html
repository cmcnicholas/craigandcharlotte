    So What Is Happening?

    You may be asking yourself... “Where is the wedding going to be?” and that is a very good question. We have decided to take our wedding abroad the Caribbean island of Cuba.

    You then might ask us... “Why go abroad?” and we would say, “because its what we want.”

    You might then say... “OK, but when, where and how much is this all going to be?” and the answer to that is, read on.

    Where

    Varadero, Cuba, you can see it on a Google Map. White sandy beaches, the Caribbean sea, Cuban culture, Varadero features in top 10 travel destinations year in, year out. You can read up about it on wikipedia.

    We will all be staying at the Paradisus Varadero Resort and Spa (click the link to see the official site) you can see what the many hundreds of reviews have to say about it on Tripadvisor. The resort is All-Inclusive so keep that in mind as your food and drink is covered in the total cost of the holiday.

    When

    Flights leave the UK (airport to be confirmed) on the 6th March 2015, travelling with Thomas Cook, returning on the 20th (sadly we do have to come back). We have a group flight booking too so we can make raucous on the way out and way in.

    How Much

    The deciding point? £1859 per person, maximum of 2 people sharing per room. An initial deposit of £200 is required on booking and I have 30 spaces available.

    If these spaces run out, don't panic we can bolt more onto the end, but the longer this is left the more chance there is that the price will go up.

    OK I'm In

    Great! Reply to us using the contact form below saying who is coming, or contact us via phone, email, text, pigeon...

    Sorry, I Can't Make It

    That's too bad if you have plans, we would both love for you to come. If it is due to the pricing then be sure to speak to Craig or Charlotte personally to see if there is anything that can be done.

    What Do We Do When We Are There?

    Relax! Family fun, eat, sleep and drink. The Resort is very large and has many things on site to keep you busy. Pools, pool bars, restaurants, games, beach, water sports, sports activities. But it doesn't just stop on the resort, the local Town of Varadero is Great to visit, Havana is only a coach ride away and there are plenty of things to go see, do and visit here are just a few including Cueva de Saturno, Coral Beach Snorkelling and Diving and the House of Rum to name a few. Some more interesting things to do can be found On this website.

    There is loads to do so please think of some ideas and we can start planning them together for people that want to do the same things. There is no pressure though and with the resort packing lots of things to do we can just stay up all night and party.
